# Copy Frameworks Reference

Detailed framework breakdowns for the copywriting skill.
Use the right framework based on the task type and audience state.

## AIDA — Attention, Interest, Desire, Action

Best for: landing pages, product pages, long-form ads

| Stage | Goal | Tactics |
|---|---|---|
| **Attention** | Stop the scroll | Bold claim, surprising stat, provocative question, pain point callout |
| **Interest** | Build curiosity | Relevant problem story, "how" teaser, relatable scenario |
| **Desire** | Make them want it | Benefits (not features), social proof, visualize the outcome |
| **Action** | Get the click/buy | Clear CTA, urgency, risk reversal (guarantee, free trial) |

### Example (EN)

> **Attention**: "Your landing page is losing 73% of visitors in the first 3 seconds."
> **Interest**: "Most pages fail because they talk about features, not outcomes."
> **Desire**: "Our clients see 2-3x conversion rates within 30 days."
> **Action**: "Start your free audit → No credit card required."

### Example (CN)

> **注意**: "你的落地页正在 3 秒内流失 73% 的访客。"
> **兴趣**: "多数页面只讲功能，不讲结果——这就是问题所在。"
> **欲望**: "我们的客户 30 天内平均转化率提升 2-3 倍。"
> **行动**: "免费领取诊断报告 → 无需绑卡"

## PAS — Problem, Agitate, Solution

Best for: pain-point aware audiences, email marketing, social ads

| Stage | Goal | Tactics |
|---|---|---|
| **Problem** | Name the pain | Describe specific, felt pain, not abstract problems |
| **Agitate** | Make it hurt more | Consequences, wasted time/money, missed opportunities |
| **Solution** | Offer relief | Your product/service as the answer, with proof |

### Example (CN — 小红书种草)

> **Problem**: "每次做 PPT 都要花 3 小时调排版？"
> **Agitate**: "同事用同样的时间做了两份方案还提前下班了。"
> **Solution**: "试试这个一键排版工具，10 分钟搞定专业级 PPT。链接在评论区👇"

---

## BAB — Before, After, Bridge

Best for: aspirational products, transformation stories, testimonials

| Stage | Goal | Tactics |
|---|---|---|
| **Before** | Current pain state | Describe the "now" vividly |
| **After** | Desired future | Paint the outcome concretely |
| **Bridge** | How to get there | Your product is the bridge |

---

## 4U — Useful, Urgent, Unique, Ultra-specific

Best for: headlines, email subject lines, ad titles

Checklist for every headline:
- [ ] **Useful** — Does it promise a clear benefit?
- [ ] **Urgent** — Is there a reason to act now?
- [ ] **Unique** — Does it stand out from competitors?
- [ ] **Ultra-specific** — Does it use numbers, names, or concrete details?

### Weak vs Strong

| Weak | Strong |
|---|---|
| "提升团队效率的好方法" | "3 个方法让你的团队会议时间减少 40%" |
| "Learn marketing" | "The 5-email sequence that generated $47K in 30 days" |
| "好用的笔记 App" | "300 万用户在用的笔记 App，启动只要 0.3 秒" |

---

## FAB — Feature, Advantage, Benefit

Best for: product descriptions, spec-to-story conversion

| Layer | Question | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Feature** | What is it? | "256GB SSD storage" |
| **Advantage** | Why is it better? | "5x faster than traditional hard drives" |
| **Benefit** | Why should I care? | "Your laptop boots in 8 seconds — no more waiting" |

### CN pattern

> 功能：AI 智能摘要
> 优势：5 秒生成一页文档的核心要点
> 利益：再也不用花 20 分钟读完整份报告了

---

## StoryBrand (simplified)

Best for: brand narratives, about pages, founder stories

1. **Character** — the customer (not you)
2. **Problem** — external, internal, philosophical
3. **Guide** — your brand (with empathy + authority)
4. **Plan** — simple 3-step process
5. **CTA** — direct + transitional
6. **Success** — what life looks like after
7. **Failure** — what happens without action (subtle stakes)

## Framework selection guide

| Scenario | First choice | Fallback |
|---|---|---|
| Landing page hero | AIDA | PAS |
| Product card / listing | FAB | 4U (headline only) |
| Cold email | PAS | BAB |
| Social media ad | PAS | BAB |
| Brand story / about page | StoryBrand | BAB |
| Email subject line | 4U | — |
| App store description | AIDA | FAB |
| 小红书种草 | PAS | BAB |
| 功能引导文案 | FAB | — |
