# Copy Quality Checklist

Self-audit every piece of copy against this checklist before delivery.
Organized by priority: P0 (must fix) → P1 (should fix) → P2 (nice to fix).

---

## P0 — Blockers (fix before delivery)

### Clarity
- [ ] Can a first-time reader understand the core message in 5 seconds?
- [ ] Is the CTA specific and actionable? ("立即领取" > "了解更多" > "点击这里")
- [ ] Does the headline deliver a clear promise or provoke curiosity?

### Truthfulness
- [ ] Are all claims verifiable? No fabricated stats, testimonials, or endorsements.
- [ ] Are results qualified appropriately? ("up to 3x" not "guaranteed 3x")
- [ ] Does the copy comply with platform advertising policies?

### Audience fit
- [ ] Is the tone matched to the target audience and platform?
- [ ] Would the target audience actually use these words?
- [ ] Is the pain point real and specific (not generic)?

---

## P1 — Craft issues (should fix)

### Hook strength
- [ ] Does the first line earn the second line?
- [ ] Is there a hook in the first 3 words (for social) or first line (for landing pages)?
- [ ] Would you stop scrolling for this?

### Benefit focus
- [ ] Benefits over features? (What it does FOR them, not what it IS)
- [ ] Is the value prop specific enough? ("Save 2 hours/week" > "Save time")
- [ ] Is there at least one concrete proof point? (number, testimonial, case)

### Flow and rhythm
- [ ] Sentence lengths vary? (No monotone cadence)
- [ ] Short paragraphs? (1-3 sentences for digital copy)
- [ ] Natural transitions between sections?

### CTA optimization
- [ ] Is there only one primary CTA? (Avoid decision paralysis)
- [ ] Does the CTA use action verbs? ("开始免费试用" > "提交")
- [ ] Is the CTA visible and logically placed?
- [ ] Is there risk reversal near the CTA? (guarantee, free trial, no commitment)

---

## P2 — Polish (nice to fix)

### AI pattern removal
- [ ] No generic filler ("在当今快速发展的时代", "In today's fast-paced world")
- [ ] No empty superlatives ("revolutionary", "game-changing", "颠覆性的")
- [ ] No mechanical list cadence (3 identical bullet structures)
- [ ] No chatbot artifacts ("希望这对您有帮助")
- [ ] No corporate buzzwords without substance ("赋能", "leverage", "synergy")

### Emotional resonance
- [ ] Does the copy make the reader feel something? (urgency, desire, relief, curiosity)
- [ ] Is social proof specific? ("1,247 teams" > "thousands of teams")
- [ ] Is fear/urgency used honestly, not manipulatively?

### Platform optimization
- [ ] Hashtags appropriate for the platform?
- [ ] Character count within limits?
- [ ] Emoji usage matches platform conventions?
- [ ] Rich text formatting (bold, line breaks) used effectively?

---

## Quick 5-dimension audit

Run this rapid check on every piece before delivery:

| Dimension | Check | Red flag |
|---|---|---|
| **Hook** | First line grab? | Generic opening, throat-clearing |
| **Clarity** | One clear message? | Multiple competing messages |
| **Proof** | Concrete evidence? | Vague claims, no specifics |
| **CTA** | Clear next step? | Missing, vague, or multiple CTAs |
| **Voice** | Sounds human? | AI-sounding, generic, corporate |
