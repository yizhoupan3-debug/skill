# Tone Presets

Match copy tone to the brand register and platform.
Each preset includes both EN and CN guidance.

---

## 1. Premium / Luxury

Use for: high-end products, luxury brands, exclusive services

**Voice**: confident, understated, aspirational

Prefer:
- elegant simplicity over flashy language
- implied exclusivity ("crafted for", "designed for those who")
- sensory and experiential language
- whitespace and brevity as design elements
- understatement over hyperbole

Reduce:
- exclamation marks
- discount/urgency language
- feature dumps
- casual or colloquial voice

### CN — 高端/奢侈调性

Prefer:
- 克制的表达，少用感叹号
- 暗示稀缺性（"为讲究的人设计"）
- 感官和体验语言
- 短句、留白

Reduce:
- 促销语气（"限时特惠"）
- 功能堆叠
- 过度热情

> ❌ "超级好用的高端手表！！！限时特惠！"
> ✅ "时间，值得更好的容器。"

---

## 2. Friendly / Approachable

Use for: consumer apps, SaaS tools, community products

**Voice**: warm, helpful, conversational, slightly playful

Prefer:
- second person ("you") and first person ("we")
- short sentences, natural rhythm
- light humor when appropriate
- questions that engage the reader
- emoji sparingly and naturally

Reduce:
- corporate stiffness
- jargon
- passive voice
- long paragraphs

### CN — 友好/平易近人调性

Prefer:
- 口语化表达（"试试看"、"超方便"）
- 短句为主
- 适度 emoji
- 直接称呼（"你"而不是"用户"）

Reduce:
- 企业腔（"致力于为您提供"）
- 被动句
- 长段落

> ❌ "本产品致力于为广大用户提供优质的使用体验。"
> ✅ "拖一下、放一下、搞定了。就这么简单 ✌️"

---

## 3. Professional / B2B

Use for: enterprise products, consulting services, B2B SaaS

**Voice**: credible, data-driven, outcome-focused

Prefer:
- data and metrics ("reduce churn by 23%")
- case study references
- clear ROI framing
- industry terminology used correctly
- structured, scannable format

Reduce:
- hype and superlatives
- casual language
- emoji
- feature-only descriptions without outcomes

### CN — 专业/B2B 调性

Prefer:
- 数据驱动（"降低 23% 的客户流失率"）
- 案例引用
- ROI 框架
- 行业术语准确使用

Reduce:
- 夸大和空洞赞美
- 过度口语化
- emoji
- 只说功能不说结果

> ❌ "我们的产品非常强大，功能丰富。"
> ✅ "已帮助 47 家中型企业将客服响应时间从 4 小时缩短至 15 分钟。"

---

## 4. Bold / Disruptive

Use for: startups, challenger brands, tech products

**Voice**: direct, opinionated, punchy, slightly provocative

Prefer:
- short declarative sentences
- contrast with the status quo
- strong verbs
- rule-breaking formatting
- manifesto energy

Reduce:
- hedging ("maybe", "possibly", "或许")
- diplomatic phrasing
- long explanations
- template structures

### CN — 大胆/颠覆调性

Prefer:
- 短句、有力
- 和现状形成对比
- 打破常规的表达
- 有态度

Reduce:
- 犹豫词（"或许"、"可能"）
- 外交辞令
- 长解释

> ❌ "我们希望能够为用户提供更好的选择。"
> ✅ "旧方式已经不行了。我们重新来过。"

---

## 5. Playful / Gen-Z

Use for: 小红书, 抖音, TikTok, youth brands, lifestyle products

**Voice**: energetic, meme-aware, emoji-rich, trend-sensitive

Prefer:
- internet slang and trending phrases (but not forced)
- emoji as punctuation and emphasis
- personal anecdote voice
- list/ranking format ("top 5 XX")
- relatable "real person" tone

Reduce:
- formal language
- brand-speak
- long paragraphs
- technical jargon

### CN — 潮流/年轻调性

Prefer:
- 网络用语（自然融入，不硬塞）
- emoji 作为节奏符号
- 个人体验叙事
- 排行榜/清单格式
- 真实感

Reduce:
- 正式语言
- 品牌官腔
- 长段落

> ❌ "本产品采用先进技术，为年轻用户提供优质体验。"
> ✅ "姐妹们！这个 App 我用了一个月，真的回不去了 😭 以前每天花 40 分钟整理笔记，现在 AI 帮我 5 分钟搞定。"

---

## 6. Empathetic / Health & Wellness

Use for: health products, mental wellness, caregiving, insurance

**Voice**: gentle, understanding, reassuring, never pushy

Prefer:
- validation of feelings
- soft CTAs ("when you're ready")
- inclusive language
- real stories over statistics
- privacy-conscious messaging

Reduce:
- urgency tactics
- fear-mongering
- clinical coldness
- before/after shame

### CN — 关怀/健康调性

Prefer:
- 理解和共情（"我们知道这不容易"）
- 温和的 CTA（"准备好了就试试看"）
- 包容性语言
- 真实故事

Reduce:
- 制造焦虑
- 冷冰冰的数据
- 对比式羞辱

---

## Tone selection guide

| Brand / Product Type | Primary tone | CTA style |
|---|---|---|
| Luxury / high-end | Premium | Invitation, not push |
| Consumer app / SaaS | Friendly | Direct, low-friction |
| Enterprise B2B | Professional | ROI-framed, demo/trial |
| Startup / challenger | Bold | Declarative, manifesto |
| Youth / lifestyle | Playful | Social, share-driven |
| Health / wellness | Empathetic | Gentle, when-ready |
