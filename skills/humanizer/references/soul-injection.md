# Soul Injection — How to Add Personality to Rewrites

Avoiding AI patterns is only half the job. Sterile, voiceless writing is just as obvious as slop.
Good writing has a human behind it.

---

## Signs of soulless writing (even if "clean")

- Every sentence is the same length and structure
- No opinions, just neutral reporting
- No acknowledgment of uncertainty or mixed feelings
- No first-person perspective when appropriate
- No humor, no edge, no personality
- Reads like a Wikipedia article or press release

---

## Techniques to add voice

### 1. Have opinions

Don't just report facts — react to them.

> ❌ "The results were interesting."
> ✅ "I genuinely don't know how to feel about this one."

### 2. Vary your rhythm

Short punchy sentences. Then longer ones that take their time getting where they're going. Mix it up.

> ❌ "The system works well. The latency is low. The throughput is high. The accuracy is acceptable."
> ✅ "The system works. Low latency, high throughput — and the accuracy is better than I expected from a first run."

### 3. Acknowledge complexity

Real humans have mixed feelings.

> ❌ "This is impressive."
> ✅ "This is impressive but also kind of unsettling."

### 4. Use first person when it fits

"I" isn't unprofessional — it's honest.

> ❌ "It can be observed that..."
> ✅ "I keep coming back to..."
> ✅ "Here's what gets me..."

### 5. Let some mess in

Perfect structure feels algorithmic. Tangents, asides, and half-formed thoughts are human.

> ❌ *Three perfectly parallel bullet points, each with identical structure.*
> ✅ "Also — and this is slightly off topic — the error messages are terrible."

### 6. Be specific about feelings

> ❌ "This is concerning."
> ✅ "There's something unsettling about agents churning away at 3am while nobody's watching."

### 7. Don't over-organize

Not everything needs a header, a list, and a summary. Let some ideas just flow within paragraphs.

### 8. Use real transitions, not template ones

> ❌ "Furthermore," / "Moreover," / "Additionally,"
> ✅ "That said," / "Here's the thing:" / "But then," / "What surprised me was..."

## Chinese-specific soul techniques / 中文灵魂注入技巧

### 1. 用口语节奏打破模板腔

> ❌ "本方案旨在全面提升团队协作效率。"
> ✅ "说白了，就是让大家少开会、少等人、少重复劳动。"

### 2. 直接说事，不绕

> ❌ "经过全面深入的调研和分析，我们发现…"
> ✅ "我们调研了三周，结论很明确：…"

### 3. 允许主观判断

> ❌ "这一方案具有一定的参考价值。"
> ✅ "这个方案有用，但不够。它只解决了表面问题。"

### 4. 不要害怕否定

> ❌ "各方面表现均达到了预期效果。"
> ✅ "速度上达标了，但准确率还差一截，特别是边界情况。"

### 5. 变化句子结构

> ❌ "X 提升了。Y 改善了。Z 增强了。"（三连式）
> ✅ "X 提升最明显，Y 也有改善。Z 倒是变化不大，可能要重新看一下方案。"

## When NOT to inject soul

- The user asked for "保留原口吻" (tone-lock mode)
- Formal legal, regulatory, or compliance text
- Scientific method sections in papers
- Technical API documentation
- Contexts where personality is genuinely inappropriate

In these cases, focus on removing AI patterns without adding personality.
Keep the prose clean, specific, and evidence-linked but don't force voice into it.

---

## Additional soul techniques

### 9. Delete quotable lines

AI writes sentences that sound ready to be tweeted or put on a slide. Humans rarely write this way outside of speeches.

> ❌ "At the end of the day, it's not about the tools — it's about the people."
> ✅ *(delete, or replace with what specifically happened next)*

If it sounds like a keynote closing line, cut it.

### 10. Trust the reader

AI explains too much because it cannot judge reader knowledge. Human authors assume competence.

> ❌ "As we all know, machine learning models require training data. This training data is used to..."
> ✅ "The model was trained on 50K labeled examples from the clinical trial dataset."

Don't explain your own metaphors. Don't define terms your audience already knows.

### 11. Detection-aware rhythm (not mechanical)

AI detectors look for uniform sentence length (low burstiness). Good writing naturally varies — you don't need to force it.

What to avoid:
- 5+ sentences of similar word count in a row
- Every paragraph being 3-5 sentences
- Every paragraph ending with a short summary sentence

What helps:
- Let some ideas be one sentence. Let others span three.
- Some paragraphs can be a single sentence if the point is sharp enough.
- End paragraphs differently — sometimes with a question, sometimes with a fact, sometimes mid-thought.

This is not about manufacturing pattern — it's about removing the AI's mechanical uniformity.

---

## The acid test

Read the rewrite aloud. Does it sound like a specific person with opinions wrote it, or does it sound like it was assembled from a database of safe phrases? If the latter, it needs more work.
